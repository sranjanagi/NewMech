Velocity analysis of four bar mechanism
An aircraft wing as a cantilever beamAn atomic force probe

Let ω2, ω3 and ω4 be the angular velocities of the links 2,3 and 4 respectively. ω2 is the
input angular velocity of the link 2. Since the link lengths a, b and c are known and
for the given θ2 value, θ3 and θ4 can be determined using position analysis. The
angular velocities ω3 and ω4 are given by

ω4=-aω2sinθ3-θ2csinθ4-θ3

ω3=-aω2sinθ4-θ2bsinθ4-θ3
